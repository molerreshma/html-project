Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana

A Grameen Kaushalya Yojana The Ministry Of Rural Development(MoRD)announced the Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana(DDU-GKY)on Antyodaya Diwas-25th September 2014.DDU-GKY is a part of the National Rural Livelihood Mission(NRLM),tasked with the dual objectives of adding diversity to the incomes of rural poor families and cater to the career aspirations of rural youth.

Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana


It was launched on 25 September 2014 by Union Ministers Nithin Gadkari and venkaiah Naidu on the occasion of 98th birth anniversary of pandit Deendayal Upadhyaya.The Vision of DDU-GKY is to"Transform rural poor youth into an economically independent and globally relevant workforce."It aims to target youth,in the age group of 15-35 years.DDU-GKY is a part of the National Rural Livelihood Mission(NRLM),tasked with the dual objectives of adding diversity to the incomes of rural poor families and cater to the career aspirations of rural youth.A corpus of Rs.1500 crore is aimed at enhancing the employability of rural youth.Under this programme,disbursements would be made through a digital voucher directly in to students bank account as part of the government's skill development initiative
